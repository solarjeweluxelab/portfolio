SolarJewel: WearOS and WatchOS

Bringing natal charts to the watch is possible thanks to the work of 3 very different groups of people who probably had not intended to be connected: Pierre Bretagnon in the 80s (astronomy), Stephen Moshier in the 90s (computing revolution) then finally Dieter Koch and Alois Treindl (astrology).

Lightweight technical accuracy

It is based on the same maths that was used to go to the moon, and today still with enough accuracy to point telescopes to and land probes on Mars.

Key Features

Real-time solar system visualization, astronomical data at a glance, customizable watch faces, and seamless integration with health and fitness tracking. Built with SwiftUI for watchOS and Jetpack Compose for WearOS.
