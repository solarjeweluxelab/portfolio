SolarJewel

Elevating my practice from curiosity to implementation.

SolarJewel is a high-precision astronomical instrument that I created from years of devoted study in UX/UI, native development, graphics, breath, meditation and astrology.

I intend for it to solve a quiet, essential need: the ability to remain present in the physical earth plane while staying aligned with the planetary flow. So if one is holding space in ceremony, in their daily flow, or just finding stillness, I want SolarJewel to opens up a portal to the heavens, right on our wrist.

Engineering the User Experience

I have always wanted to be a UX Engineer and spent years training in it and my dream has finally came true. This is both a tech jewel for the soul, but also a professional astrology engine.

Tech-jewlery for the soul

My aim was to achieve lightweight beauty but with the deep and uncompromising functionality of a professional astrology engine. And in a casing that is safe and protective during delicate tasks.

Quiet Companion

It is intended to serve as a quiet companion that provides glanceable enchantment while keeping our hands free for the work of living. So a dedicated partner for those who seek to walk between worlds.

A Precision Gradient

I have always loved rainbows, so this jewel toned scientific color palette has been tuned for optical harmony to ensure that every sign glows with a balanced and sophisticated light.

User Experience

Step back or shift your perspective instantly. Sweep from Natal, Transit to Secondary Progression and Solar Arc with a vertical swipe.

The Professional's Secret

Compare ancient and modern house systems (Placidus, Porphyry, and several others) with a horizontal swipe.

Luminous Time-Travel

Use swipe gestures to also move through time. You can revisit the planetary geometry of major life events or look forward to see how the "now" informs your path.

Sacred Privacy, Scientific Truth and Total Sanctuary

Sacred Privacy, Scientific Truth and Total Sanctuary: In this tool, our birth and transit data never leave your wrist; every complex chart calculation is performed locally on your device. These planets and aspects are yours alone.
